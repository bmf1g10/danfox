Case Study

TINE Handel

I updated and improved the e-commerce website to Norwegian dairy company TINE Handel.I worked as part of a team and followed the Design Thinking Process.Below is a very general overview:

Empathy
Identified user research goals, planned and carried out primary and secondary research including interviews, a literature review and metadata. I then analysed results through affinity mapping and atomic research to find insights and make recommendations.

Define
Created personas, made a user scenario and formulated a problem statement.

Ideation
Planned and carried out ideation-workshops using techniques; brainstorming, sketchstorming, SCAMPER and MOSCOW. Created low fidelity prototypes and wireframes for both mobile and desktop interfaces. Considered user navigation and possible user journeys. Created wireflows and userflows.

Prototyping
Used Figma to create medium and high-fidelity prototypes for mobile and desktop interfaces.

Testing
Planned and carried out usability testing. Analysed results and created a usability testing report where recommendations were made to the next steps in developing the product.

Learning points
This was a large project and I worked thoroughly throughout.Â  As such I increased my understanding of, and ability to carry out, (the areas within) each stage of the design thinking process. The experience also made me more proficient in using Figma and I feel confident in creating new designs. Responsive design was also a key design solution to this project and I feel me knowledge of the use of responsive design has also improved. Also I have learnt the importance of a first audit to existing designs and, in regards to testing, making advance contact with good time in recruiting participants.

About Me

I'm a professional UX designer

I am Daniel, a recently qualified UX-Designer with some working experience. I live in Bergen, Norway. I have a professional degree in UX-Design and achieved the highest grades and good feedback. In UX-Design I have found my passion and believe in the Design Thinking Process. My largest project to date was in cooperation with TINE Handel, Norway's largest dairy producer, where I worked in a team and redesigned their e-commerce website. I am currently working voluntarily on redesigning an application that helps doctors manage the medication of patients with Parkinsons Disease. My working background is as a nursery projectworker and a police officer. In these professions I have been in regular contact with different people with very different needs and motivations. This has given me a good grounding for understanding people and I have also studied empathy as part of my Child -and Youth qualification.

How I work

I am used to, and enjoy work best as part of a team. I am confident and can work well on my own. I have leant how useful it is to discuss and share information with other UX-Designers who have slightly different perspectives while also having confidence in my own knowledge and ideas. My passion is user-centred design, to create solutions that are intuitive for the user and inclusive for the general public. I use good UX practices, principles and design systems to create consistent and clear designs.
